## What is Goalst?

Goalst is a goal tracking platform that helps you achieve what matters most by breaking down big ambitions into manageable sub-goals. Track your progress, earn achievement ranks, and stay motivated with a clean, intuitive interface.

Whether you're planning a fitness transformation, launching a project, or organizing a trip, Goalst gives you the structure and motivation to see it through.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your first goal">
    Give your goal a title, description, and deadline. Set a priority level to determine how many points you'll earn when you complete it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Break it down">
    Add sub-goals to break down your main goal into actionable steps. You can nest sub-goals as deep as you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your progress">
    Check off sub-goals as you complete them. Your main goal's progress updates automatically based on what you've finished.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Level up">
    Complete goals to earn score points. Accumulate points to unlock higher ranks and celebrate your achievements.
  </Step>
</Steps>
